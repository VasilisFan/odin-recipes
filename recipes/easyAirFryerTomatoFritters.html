Easy air fryer tomato fritters

Description

Prepare this classic Greek delicacy without deep frying it! Juicy on the inside and crispy on the outside, these tomato fritters are loaded with feta and herbs for a yummy summer appetizer!

Ingredients

  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 1 spring onion
  • ¼ bunch parsley
  • 2 tablespoon(s) mint, fresh
  • ½ teaspoon(s) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon(s) oregano, dried
  • 200 g self-rising flour
  • 150 g feta cheese
  • 1-2 tablespoon(s) olive oil, + extra for the air fryer's basket
  • 60 g dry breadcrumbs
  • salt
  • pepper

    • To serve

    • rocket

    Steps

    Let's get cooking...

    1. Line the air fryer’s basket with parchment paper.
    2. Put the canned tomatoes into a bowl.
    3. Finely chop the spring onion, parsley, and mint, and add them to the bowl.
    4. Season with salt, pepper, sugar, and oregano, and mix the ingredients with a spoon.
    5. Add the flour, crumble the feta cheese with your hands into the bowl, and mix well.
    6. Add the olive oil, and mix again.
    7. Put the dried breadcrumbs into a bowl, and set it aside.
    8. Take a spoonful of the mixture and transfer it to the dried breadcrumbs.
    9. Make sure it is completely coated with the breadcrumbs, and shape it gently with your hands into an oval fritter.
    10. Follow the same process until you have 20 tomato fritters in total.
    11. Transfer the first batch (about 7 tomato fritters) to the air fryer’s basket, and drizzle the fritters with a little olive oil.
    12. Cook them at 200°C (390°F) for 8-10 minutes, and follow the same process for the remaining 2 batches.
    13. Serve the tomato fritters with rocket.
