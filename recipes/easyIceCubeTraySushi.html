Easy ice cube tray sushi

Description

Steps

For the marinade

  1. Put the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt into a bowl, whisk them well until the sugar and salt are dissolved, and set the marinade aside.

For the rice

  1. Pour cold water into a bowl and then place a sieve over it.
  2. Put the rice into the sieve and stir in circular motions until the starch is released and the water turns white.
  3. Drain the water from the bowl, refill with clean water, and follow the same process 4-5 more times until the water runs clear and most of the starch has been removed from the rice.
  4. Transfer the rice to the bowl, cover it with water, and set it aside for 15 minutes.
  5. Drain the rice into a sieve, place it back into the bowl, and add 300 g of water.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave it for 10 minutes at 800 Watts.
  7. Remove the bowl from the microwave, take out the plastic wrap, and mix the rice with a wooden spoon.
  8. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap again, and set it aside for another 10 minutes.
  9. Pour the marinade into the bowl and mix well.
  10. Transfer the rice to a baking pan and spread it over the whole surface.
  11. Cover the pan with a wet towel, gently press it down so that it rests directly on the rice, and set the pan aside to cool well.

To assemble

  1. Line two 14-slot plastic ice cube trays with plastic wrap, pressing it down so it fits into each compartment.
  2. Cut the salmon fillet into 8 thin slices, the shrimp into 6 very small pieces, the surimi in half, the avocado into 10 thin slices, the carrot and cucumber into very thin, small sticks, and the seaweed sheet into 28 small squares.
  3. Transfer the cream cheese to a pastry bag, cutting off the tip, and then the mayonnaise to a squeeze bottle.
  4. Place a small piece of salmon into 8 of the compartments and pipe the cream cheese on top.
  5. In another 10 compartments, divide the avocado and the carrot and cucumber sticks.
  6. In 6 more compartments, divide the shrimp and top with mayonnaise. In the remaining 4 compartments, divide the surimi and the rest of the mayonnaise.
  7. Wet your hands with water, divide the rice among all the compartments, and press gently with your hands.
  8. Place a small piece of nori seaweed on top of each compartment.
  9. You can wrap the tray with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for up to one day.Alternatively, unmold and serve immediately by inverting the tray onto a serving board.

For the decoration

  1. Add a little mayonnaise to all the sushi rolls except those with salmon.
  2. To the salmon sushi rolls, add a bit of cream cheese.
  3. Garnish with flying fish roe, spring onion, black sesame seeds, or lime zest.
  4. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi paste, and pickled ginger.

Ingredients

For the marinade

  • 25 g rice vinegar
  • 15 g granulated sugar
  • 5 g salt

For the rice

  • 250 g rice, for sushi
  • 300 g water

To assemble

  • 150 g salmon fillet
  • 4 shrimps, cleaned and boiled
  • 4 stick(s) surimi
  • 1/3 avocado(s)
  • 1/3 carrot
  • 1/3 cucumber
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 100 g mayonnaise
  • 1 seaweed, nori

For the decoration

  • mayonnaise
  • cream cheese
  • flying fish roe
  • spring onion
  • sesame seeds, black
  • lime zest

To serve

  • soy sauce
  • wasabi paste
  • ginger, pickled
